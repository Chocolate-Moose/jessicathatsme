studibuddi

Tinder, but for study buddies!

Context

Made with a high school friend, as a fun side project

Time Frame

3 months

Tech Stacks

React JS

Firebase

Team

Jessica Cheng - Designer, Developer

Jennifer Guo - Designer, Developer

My friend and I came up with this idea after noticing how hard it was to find project partners and study buddies for classes, especially with everything being remote. We were inspired to create StudiBuddi -- Tinder, but for finding study buddies and project partners.

Users first create an account, where they are asked a series of questions about how they work. We then allow them to swipe right or left on the profiles of other people in their classes and also looking for a study buddy! If two users match, they will recieve each others contact information so they can start studying together.
